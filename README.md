Phase 1: ASK (Defining the Business Goal)
In this phase, we act as the Lead Data Analyst for a fictional gym chain called "Apex Fitness." Management has noticed some members are dropping out, and they want to know who is leaving and why.

1.1 The Business Task
Identify key trends and patterns in member behavior to predict which members are at risk of leaving (churning) and provide data-driven recommendations to improve gym retention.

Reason: This transforms "looking at numbers" into "solving a business problem" that saves the company money.

1.2 Key Stakeholders
Gym Manager: Needs to know which classes or equipment to invest in.

Marketing Team: Needs to know which age groups to target with promotions.

You (The Analyst): Responsible for finding the insights in the data.

1.3 The Core Questions we will answer:
Demographics: Does a specific Age group or Gender have lower attendance?

Habits: Is there a correlation between Workout_Type (e.g., Yoga vs. HIIT) and the number of sessions per week?

Physical Stats: Do members with higher BMI or lower Experience_Level tend to drop out faster?

Financial Impact: How does Session_Duration relate to the "value" the member gets from their membership?
